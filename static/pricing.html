How token pricing works

Everything you see here updates live from the platform's current settings — the same numbers used everywhere in SkillCoach.

1What is a token?

AI models read and write text in small pieces called tokens — roughly ¾ of an English word each. "Coaching helps businesses grow" is about 6 tokens. Every message you send and every reply you receive consumes tokens from your credit balance.

2Input tokens vs output tokens

There are two kinds, and they cost different amounts:

Input tokens (cheaper) — everything the AI has to read before answering: your message, plus the whole conversation so far, plus the coaching skill's instructions. Because the AI re-reads the full history on every turn, long conversations use a lot of input tokens.
Output tokens (costlier) — the words the AI writes back to you.

3Current model rates

Rates are in US dollars per 1 million tokens, straight from Anthropic's (the AI provider's) published pricing. The blended cost combines the input and output rates using the split above — it's the practical "what does 1 million of my credit tokens cost" number.

4A worked example

Balance values shown across SkillCoach (the "≈ $" figures) always assume the cheapest model — so your balance is worth at least what's shown. If you chat on a pricier model, the same balance simply runs out sooner; the app shows you a warning whenever you pick one.

5Where your usage is recorded

Every message's exact token usage is stored permanently. Your monthly usage report shows the precise dollar cost of what you actually consumed — real tokens, real models, no estimates. Ask your coach or the Head Coach for your usage report at any time.
